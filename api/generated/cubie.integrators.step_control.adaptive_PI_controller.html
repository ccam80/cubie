Initialise a proportional–integral step controller.

Parameters:

  • precision – Precision used for controller calculations.

  • dt_min – Minimum allowed step size.

  • dt_max – Maximum allowed step size.

  • atol – Absolute tolerance specification.

  • rtol – Relative tolerance specification.

  • algorithm_order – Order of the integration algorithm.

  • n – Number of state variables.

  • kp – Proportional gain before scaling for controller order.

  • ki – Integral gain before scaling for controller order.

  • min_gain – Lower bound for the step size change factor.

  • max_gain – Upper bound for the step size change factor.

  • deadband_min – Lower gain threshold for holding the previous step size.

  • deadband_max – Upper gain threshold for holding the previous step size.
